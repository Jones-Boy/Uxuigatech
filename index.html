About

Hi, I am Michael Jones a software engineer and User interface designer.

I specialize in user research, idea presentation and mobile design. My most proficient coding languages are Ruby, followed by Javascript and Html. I enjoy meeting and working with stakeholders to find out the best way to create useful products or improve current ones.

I have worked in over 44 countries and have been around a wide variety of cultures that have given me a unique insight and view of potential joy, pain points, needs and desires.

My Ideal Client would be a forward thinking client with a desire to make a positive impact threw creation or concept.
